# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement
George T. Smith is a University of Iowa student graduating in May 2026 with a BBA in Business Analytics & Information Systems and a minor in Sports Management. He will be attending law school in the fall and aims for a career in sports law, specifically in-house legal roles within sports organizations.  

The problem this landing page solves is that George’s resume alone does not clearly communicate his **career path toward law**, his **interest in sports**, or his **competitive, caring, and focused personality**. The landing page provides a professional online presence that highlights both his personal and professional identity.

---

## 02 — Target Audience
**Primary audience:**  
- General professional networking audience (connections, employers, professors, professionals, peers)

**Secondary audience:**  
- Sports organizations and legal professionals who may come across the profile

**What they are looking for:**  
- George’s career direction toward law and sports  
- His personality and professionalism  
- Academic and work experience  
- Interests, goals, and contact information

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|-----------------|
| Increase visibility and professional presence | 50–100 page visits |
| Build connections with professional contacts | 3–5 LinkedIn connection requests |
| Spark conversations about sports law or career opportunities | 1–2 professional conversations |
| Maintain a curated, accessible online presence | General online presence (visitors can learn about background and interests) |

**Bio**
- Must: Academic status, major, graduation date, career interests

**Profile Photo**
- Must: Include headshot stored locally in `images/` folder

**Skills**
